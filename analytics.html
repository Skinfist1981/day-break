  • Interactive graphs

    See how many emails were delivered, how many people opened your email, what the click percentage is and much more. Get an overview of your list growth, know the location of your subscribers and track engagement by country.

    Discover which email addresses bounced and why, and we'll help you in the decision whether they should be deleted from your list or no. Know the email addresses that unsubscribed from your list and prevent their addition to your list again.
